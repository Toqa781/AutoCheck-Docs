HPCStateTracker

Tracks elapsed wall-clock time and job phase for long-running HPC simulation jobs.

Relevant source files testing/ simulation_output.txt

Tracked fields

FieldTypeDescription
elapsed_secondsfloatWall-clock time since the job started, independent of any training-specific loop structure.
phasestrA coarse label for the current job stage, useful for HPC simulations that don't have epochs in the ML sense.

Why HPC jobs need a different tracker

Not every long-running HPC job has the epoch/batch structure of ML training. Simulation jobs may run for a fixed wall-clock duration with no natural step boundaries. HPCStateTracker reflects that by anchoring entirely on elapsed time rather than iteration counts.
